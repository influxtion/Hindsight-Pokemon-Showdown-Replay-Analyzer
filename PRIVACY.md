# Hindsight — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 11, 2026*

Hindsight ("the extension") is a browser extension that analyzes Pokémon
Showdown replays and displays statistics on the replay page.

## Data collection

Hindsight does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. It does
not use analytics, tracking, cookies, or remote servers.

## How it works

When you open a page on `https://replay.pokemonshowdown.com`, Hindsight reads
the replay's battle log that is already present on that page, analyzes it
locally within your browser, and renders the results on the page. This data
never leaves your device and is not persisted after you close the page.

## Permissions

The extension requests access only to `https://replay.pokemonshowdown.com`
pages, solely to read replay data and display its analysis. It requests no
other permissions and accesses no other websites.
